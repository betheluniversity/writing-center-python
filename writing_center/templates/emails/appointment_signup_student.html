Additional Information:

For our online Writing Center appointments, we will be using Google Docs and Zoom.

Google Docs

Five minutes before the start of your appointment, please Share your Google Doc with your tutor. Make sure they have permission to Edit the document.

Zoom

During the time and day of your scheduled Writing Center appointment, you can view and enter the Zoom session via the Zoom link when you view your session here, or you can view the Zoom url in the reminder email sent yesterday.

Please follow these steps to connect with them:

  1. Open the Zoom meeting and wait for the meeting to begin.
  2. If the meeting has already begun, wait for your tutor to move you from the waiting room to the meeting.
  3. Please start this process 5 minutes before your appointed time to make sure your connection and equipment are all working. Also, make sure video and audio are on (or whatever your preferences are).
